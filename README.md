# Text-Based-Adventure-Game
Simple text based game in which you battle goblins, trolls, and dragons at random.

The game starts by asking your name, then prompts the user to input c to start moving. 

Once the adventurer is on the move, he is confronted by enemies. The player has 150 health and 5 damage, with a 25% chance of dealing critical damage (10 dmg).

Enemies:

- Goblin, a 10 health, 5 damage creature who has a 90% chance of dealing critical damage to the player, doubling his damage output for one hit. He has a 50% chance of appearing and will thus be the most common enemy you will face.

- Troll, a 15 health, 8 damage monster who has a 10% critical chance. They spawn in at a 35% chance.

- Dragon, a 30 health, 20 damage colossus who has a 5% critical chance (Thank god!). They have a 15% spawn chance, stand ready for your greatest quest yet, brave adventurer!
